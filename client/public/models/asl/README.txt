Place your ASL TFJS models here.

Expected files:
- word/model.json + weight shards
- word/labels.json (array of words)
- alpha/model.json + weight shards
- alpha/labels.json (array of letters)

This repo ships a starter alphabet model under alpha/ (Sign Language MNIST).
Word models are not bundled.

You can also point to hosted files using the VITE_ASL_* env variables.
